About

We are three Computer Science students of the first year and we decided to create a webpage for electric car owners. We aim to facilitate the users daily time management connected to recharging the vehicle. Therefore, we created ChargeHub. It is a website that displays all available charging stations for electric vehicles in a given area, in addition to calculating when and where it is necessary to recharge the vehicle during a long journey. This website can be used to plan the user's trips by finding appropriate charging stations along the way. There will be the possibility to open a map which displays the current location of the user, along with the charging stations in the given area. When signing up the user gives us important information, including the vehicles he owns. This helps our algorithm determine the possible distance the vehicle can cover with a loaded battery. To plan a trip our algorithm implements the length of the route, all nearby stations and the vehicles owned by the user. We stand for a greener future and plan on contributing to it.
